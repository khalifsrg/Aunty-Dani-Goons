What does it do? (600 words) What is the state of the art of this new technology? What can be done now? What is likely to be able to do be done soon (say in the next 3 years)? What technological or other developments make this possible?

Cybersecurity is one of the most rapidly growing fields at this current time. It is the development of structures that ensure the protection as well as safety of many things, for the most part it is commonly in regard to ethics and crime. The state-of-the-art technology is the process of preventing or recovering programs, devices and networks from any types of cybersecurity attacks. These cyber-attacks are most commonly conducted in order to access data, with the intention of exploitation. Another very common intention behind these attacks is to steal money from the innocent or major corporations. These cyber threats are actively developing and evolving into major issues. This explains the rapid growth of the cybersecurity field. These threats can lead to the demise and destruction of all businesses alike, small or large. It can be very financially damaging if the correct pre-cautions are not taken.

At this current time, one of the most used means of cybersecurity is a Virtual Private Network, also known as ‘VPN’ for short. Used by millions of people around the world, the concept is quite simple. It is in the name, where users connect to the internet via their own private network, rather than using public networks. Immediately, this is preventing their data from being accessed publicly by external entities. The VPN provides anonymity as well as online privacy when browsing the web (eg. History, cookies, passwords, etc). More specifically, the VPN masks your Internet Protocol also known as ‘IP’ address. It does this by assigning a foreign IP to your network, making your actual IP practically untraceable. Encryption allows daily activities such as online shopping or social media browsing to become a safer practice by providing peace of mind to users that their data is not easily accessible by literally anyone in the public.

One of the hottest emerging cybersecurity technologies is called User-behaviour analytics. It in a sense records a user’s daily activity and behavioural patterns, then based off that determines whether or not certain activity could potentially be malicious or unintentional. This is done through deep analytics, where the system creates a comparison between the user’s past behaviour and their present. This is a very interesting development for the cybersecurity field as in the past, or up to now, it has always been that once an attacker gets the certain user’s credentials correct and gains access to their account or network, that’s that. They have full control and can do as they please. However, with this new User-behaviour analytics technology, once the attacker has a hold of the enterprise, if they can even access it at all, they still have a very high chance of being rejected or removed as the system is always monitoring the users behaviour for any irregularities.

There are many close-knit fields that support the development of cybersecurity greatly. Some immediate examples are cloud-computing and the development of block-chain based systems. It is believed that the development of cloud-computing will have a tremendous impact of the cyber-security field. It will open up many job opportunities as well as room for further development of security infrastructures that are far more secure than what we already have. Many of the current cyber-security technologies for example, firewalls may transition over to cloud based in the near future, making it virtualized and much stronger. The block-chain model is also one of the latest developments in the cyber-security field. It is said to provide security by checking and verifying the identity of both sides within a transaction. The structure of blockchains also makes it almost impossible for attackers to access the internal networks.

It is a digital form of currency, with no connection nor relationship with any major banks. The major feature within the development of crypto currencies is the wealth of safety that is backed up by it, many people now adays are transitioning into using crypto currencies as the payments are extremely secure and are digital entries that are stored into online databases the describe the said transactions. More specifically, crypto currency transactions are encrypted and backed by very advanced coding for the storage of the currencies. The main aim of the rapidly developing field is to provide a safer and more secure means of spending your money. Crypto-currencies are built into the block-chain model, a very new emerging field of technology. These blockchains represent the way in which transactions are documented, where they are segmented into blocks and given a time stamp for when the transaction occurred. These blocks back it almost impossible for any hackers to access or any potential malicious activities to occur.

Another layer of added protection in this service is that most crypto currency platforms will almost always have a two-factor authentication feature which would be linked to either your mobile phone or personal email. This makes it much more secure and harder for any attackers to access your finances. However, although it may be extremely secure in some aspects, it is not at all 100% impenetrable. Incidents like this event, which happened in 2018, is what makes the new upcoming crypto currency seem so foreign for the average person. Project Idea Plant'd

Plant’d – The indoor gardening application

Our team project idea is an app that targets consumers looking to decorate their home with indoor plants. Money and time can be wasted by selecting a plant that isn't compatible for the space in which you'd like to plant it in. This app is created to educate, help and save users money and time in the process of decorating and designing. The motivation behind designing this app comes from personal experiences of myself and those around me particularly during the pandemic. During 2020, masses of work forces were sent to work from home, but many lacked the ability to do so efficiently. Having plants, indoor or out, can help with boost productivity, help with creative thinking, concentration and attentiveness. Not to mention the health advantages of having indoor plants can include cleaner air, reducing stress levels and blood pressure levels.

Our aim, to create an app that made choosing plants easy, simple and hassle free. The name of our app is called Plant’d. Plant’d helps users with design placement and makes ordering the plants stress free through a few clicks of a button. We are partnered with wholesale nurseries to help support businesses outside of gardening chains such as Bunnings. This also means that our users have access to discounted plants all while supporting more independent businesses. The educates user on how to correctly care for their plants and allow them to flourish. We have included features designed to help identify symptoms of sick plants and informs users on how to nurse them back to health. We have also included discussion forum for users to share ideas, provide advice and ask questions to a community of green thumbs.

Our demographic of users will be users between 25-40, they are tech savvy and live busy lifestyles. We aim to provide a smooth process from start to finish that will allow users to create mock-ups that will help the user better visualise their design before purchasing. The app will require its users to have a baseline knowledge of app usage and the ability to navigate their way around applications. Plant’d has a simplistic and easy to read design allowing for positive user experience.

After looking into the market I could only find applications that allowed users to better look after their plant. There is nothing that actually provides users with a visual aid on how plant placement would look in their space and then connects those users to wholesale suppliers.

Description In order to download the app, users will require a handheld device with a built-in camera, the ability to download applications as well as an internet connection whether it be via a phone network or Wi-Fi, a smartphone or a tablet. Other equipment users will need, although it's not essential before downloading, is light gardening equipment. The option of purchasing equipment prior to checkout is available and will of course require a debit or credit card to finalise their order.

Once downloaded and opened, the app will ask the user if they are new to the app or if they’d like to register as a new user. Once the registration stage has been completed and the user is logged in, a 'Home' will show. This page will consist of a list of buttons ie. 'Home', 'Design', ‘Health', ‘Suppliers', 'Plant a Question'.

Design - This button is for the users to select when they are ready to take a photo and design. Once clicked, the app will open a camera and want to take a photo of the desired area. Once the photo has been taken, a list of plants will pop up on the right-hand side of the screen (over the photo). The user can then click and drag the plants they want to try over the photo as a mock-up. They are also able to resize the plant to better fit. If they'd like to remove a plant, they can click it and a small 'x' will appear to delete. Once the user has decorated the photo of the room to their liking, they can then click ‘Confirm’, this will guide them to a page where they will check the plants and their pot sizes as well as each plant’s price. Users can also purchase gardening equipment if required for installation. Once their selection is confirmed, the app will guide them through the purchase process with the supplier, they will be able to select delivery or pick-up followed by payment method (credit card, PayPal etc). Upon completion, the user will be sent a tax invoice and order confirmation email and the supplier will be notified to work on and dispatch the order.

Health - This tab is designed to help users determine what is wrong with sick plants they own. Through this section users can search for the plant (if they know the name) or browse images to id the plant. Once located, they can identify what is wrong with the plant and how to remedy it back to health. Whether the plant is sick due to over-watering, under-watering, too much sun, not enough or from an illness/infestation. All illnesses are accompanied by photos to make the process easier as well as recommendations on any products that may help the process. There is also a blurb per plant including their scientific name and basic instructions for care.

Suppliers - This page is a list of all suppliers that have partnered with the app that users purchase plants through. They are wholesale nurseries who offer delivery to the app’s users. In this page, users can find contact information and addresses if they prefer to go and browse plants themselves. This page is similar to that of Google Maps – Displaying all of the business information as well as a visual location on a map.

Plant a question – This section of the app is a discussion forum that allows interaction between users. They can ask questions, provide tips and tricks and receive advice. Included is a search bar to find if a topic has been previously discussed/ question has been answered. Similar to discord, users can join servers/threads on certain topics, create groups and add other users as friends for private chats.
